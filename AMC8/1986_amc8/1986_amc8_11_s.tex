

If $ A \star B$ means $ \frac{A+B}{2}$, then $ (3 \star 5) \star 8$ is

\[ \textbf{(A)}\ 6 \qquad
\textbf{(B)}\ 8 \qquad
\textbf{(C)}\ 12 \qquad
\textbf{(D)}\ 16 \qquad
\textbf{(E)}\ 30
\]
\\
Solution
\\
We just plug in and evaluate: \begin{align*} (3*5)*8 &= \left( \frac{3+5}{2}\right) *8 \\ &= 4*8 \\ &= \frac{4+8}{2} \\ &= 6 \\ \end{align*}
$\boxed{\text{A}}$
