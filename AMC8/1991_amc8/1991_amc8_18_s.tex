\\
Solution
\\
Let $x$ be the amount of people each $\text{X}$ represents. The percent of people that worked for five or more years is then \[\frac{2x+2x+2x+x+x+x}{5x+5x+8x+3x+2x+2x+2x+x+x+x}=30\% \rightarrow \boxed{\text{C}}.\]
