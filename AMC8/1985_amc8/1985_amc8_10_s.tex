\\
Solution
\\
The fraction halfway between $\frac{1}{5}$ and $\frac{1}{3}$ is simply their average, which is \begin{align*} \frac{\frac{1}{5}+\frac{1}{3}}{2} &= \frac{\frac{3}{15}+\frac{5}{15}}{2} \\ &= \frac{\frac{8}{15}}{2} \\ &= \frac{4}{15} \\ \end{align*}
